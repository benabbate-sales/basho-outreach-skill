# Prospect Outreach — Basho-Style Template

**Use:** Cold outreach to a decision-maker at an ICP firm. Goal is a 20-minute discovery call, not a pitch.
**Style:** Basho framework adapted for enterprise buyers. Every sentence earns its place. No vendor-speak.

---

## TEMPLATE

**Subject:** [Specific public signal — e.g. "Your Q3 comments on [topic]" or "[Industry shift] and its impact on [their function]"]

Hi [First name],

[Specific public signal, 1–2 sentences. Earnings call mention, a piece they published, a hiring pattern, a regulatory change affecting them specifically, or a recent M&A event. Show you've read it. No compliments.]

I'm [your role] at [your firm]. We help firms like [Client A], [Client B], and [Client C] [one-line outcome — what you actually do for them, stated measurably]. Typically we find [conservative, verifiable outcome — e.g. "a meaningful percentage of [category] spend that's unused or duplicated," not "massive savings"].

I'm not pitching a demo. I'd like 20 minutes to compare notes on how [Recipient firm] is handling [specific pressure named in paragraph 1]. Does [specific day/time, e.g. "Tuesday at 2pm"] work, or would later in the week suit?

Best,
[Your first name]

---

## SECTION-BY-SECTION NOTES

**Subject.** Mirror the specific public signal. Avoid "Quick question" or anything that sounds like mass send.

**Hook.** Decision-makers get dozens of vendor emails a week. The only way through is proving you've read something specific about their firm. Never "I saw your firm is doing well."

**Credibility + why-now.** Named clients do more work than adjectives. Use 2–3 accounts safe to cite (see `CONFIG.md`). The outcome claim must be measured — the rule is: never overstate what your firm delivers. "A meaningful percentage" beats "huge savings."

**CTA.** The phrase "I'm not pitching a demo" lowers the defence. Propose a real slot. 20 minutes is the ceiling.

---

## VARIABLES TO FILL EACH SEND

- `[First name]`
- `[Recipient firm]`
- `[Specific public signal]` ← must be verifiable and recent
- `[Your role]`, `[your firm]`, `[Client A], [Client B], [Client C]` ← from `CONFIG.md`, ideally peers of the prospect's firm
- `[One-line outcome]` ← your standard value statement
- `[Specific pressure named in paragraph 1]`
- `[Specific day/time]`
- `[Your first name]`

---

## RULES

1. Public signal must be < 90 days old. Older = stale = obvious template.
2. Named clients should be peers of the prospect's firm.
3. Never claim a specific savings % unless the user has verified it for this prospect.
4. One CTA only. If a second ask creeps in, cut it.
