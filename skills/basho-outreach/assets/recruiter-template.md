# Recruiter Outreach — Basho-Style Template

**Use:** Cold/warm outreach to recruiters in your sector. Goal is to open a long-term relationship, not chase a specific role.
**Style:** Adapted from John Barrows' Basho framework. Personalised hook → credibility → relevance → low-friction meeting ask.

---

## TEMPLATE

**Subject:** [Recruiter's stated focus — e.g. "Your work placing enterprise SaaS sales leaders in EMEA"]

Hi [First name],

I've been following your work at [Recruiter firm] for a while — particularly [specific placement, post, or sector focus, e.g. "your recent piece on the EMEA fintech sales market" or "the senior commercial roles you've run for [named client]"]. It's clear you specialise in the part of the market I operate in.

A bit of context on me: I'm [your role] at [your firm], [one-line description of what the firm does and who it serves]. I've spent [X years] building and leading [what you build/lead] in [region/sector], and I'm at the stage where it's worth keeping a small group of trusted recruiters close — people who know the market and the kind of roles that would genuinely fit.

I'm not actively looking, but I'd value 20 minutes to introduce myself properly and understand the kind of mandates you're running. Are you free [specific day/time, e.g. "Thursday at 3pm"], or would early next week suit better?

Best,
[Your first name]

---

## SECTION-BY-SECTION NOTES

**Subject line.** Mirror something the recruiter has publicly said or done. Generic = deleted. If you can't find a specific angle, do more research before sending.

**Hook (para 1).** Reference one specific thing — a placement, a post, a sector. Proves you've done the work. No "I hope this finds you well."

**Credibility + relevance (para 2).** Two jobs: (1) establish you're worth their time via your role, firm, and — if applicable — named clients pulled from `CONFIG.md`; (2) explain *why now* — you're being deliberate about who you keep in your network.

**CTA (para 3).** Direct meeting ask with a specific time. Easier to say yes/no to a proposal than to "let me know when works." Keep it to 20 minutes — low commitment.

## VARIABLES TO FILL EACH SEND

- `[First name]`
- `[Recruiter firm]`
- `[Specific placement / post / sector focus]` ← do the LinkedIn research
- `[Your role]`, `[your firm]`, `[one-line firm description]` ← from `CONFIG.md`
- `[X years]` ← tailor to the recruiter's seniority focus
- `[Region/sector]` ← match to their patch
- `[Specific day/time]` ← always propose a real slot
- `[Your first name]`

---

## RULES

1. Never send without filling every variable. If you can't fill one, the recipient isn't worth contacting yet.
2. One recruiter at a time. This is not a sequence.
3. If they don't reply in 5 working days, one short follow-up — then leave it.
